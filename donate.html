Our Key Principles

  1. Transparency

    We maintain open communication about how funds are allocated and the outcomes achieved.

  2. Prioritization

    We assess the needs of our families first and direct funds to areas where they can make the most difference.

  3. Impact Assessment

    We regularly evaluate the effectiveness of our spending to ensure that we are achieving our goals and making the best use of donor contributions.
